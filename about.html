You Know About Us!

Your Partner in Security, Manpower & Sanitation.

Kate Manpower Services India Private Limited (KMSIPL) is a trusted and professionally managed organization committed to delivering reliable manpower, security, and sanitation solutions across India. With years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation for quality service, operational excellence, and customer satisfaction.

We specialize in providing trained security personnel, skilled and unskilled manpower, and innovative sanitation solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our team is carefully selected, thoroughly trained, and continuously monitored to ensure the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and professionalism.

Vision

To create a safer, cleaner, and more sustainable India by delivering reliable security, manpower, and sanitation solutions that empower communities and build lasting trust.

Mission

To provide professional security, skilled manpower, and innovative sanitation services through technology, ethical practices, and commitment to safety, quality, and customer satisfaction.
